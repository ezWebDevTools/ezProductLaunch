ezProductLaunch

Responsive single-page Bootstrap 3.0 theme

A boilerplate template crafted to be minimum viable packaging for your repo'ed product. A low-frills yet uber-thorough philosophy enables you to properly showcase all your product's attributes, broaden your brand, and make fork'ers forkin' happy.

Overview

Less thinking. More doing. You understand that quest, yes?

The ultimate minimum viable packaging for your software product. A lean and clean design that more importantly is highly usable and reusable. A go-to power tool you can lean on when time is tight, details are scattered and marketing matters.

But wait, there's more!

Specially crafted to serve double duty as an easy to follow checklist that pulls together most everything your product's packaging should include.

You worked hard to build your product. Now tighten it up. Wrap a ribbon around it. Slap on this bow. Ship it.

Fame awaits you.

Key Benefits & Features:

  • Intentionally Lovely & Minimal
    Easily trick it out to match your brand's identity.

  • Consistent UX Across All Screen Sizes
    The IA and UI has been thoroughly distilled and optimized.

  • Using ezProductLaunch Makes You Smarter
    Follow the TODOs. Flesh out the Start Here sections. Done!

  • It's Fast & Lean
    Bootstrap 3.x (CSS and js), as well as Web Fonts and the jQuery library are all served via CDN.

Saves time. Looks good. What's not to love?
